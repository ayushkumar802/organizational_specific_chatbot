# **Topic 4: Leave & Attendance Policy**

---

## **4.1 Types of Leave**

XYZ Company provides different types of leave to support employee well-being and work-life balance.

1. **Casual Leave (CL)**

   * Granted for personal reasons or short-term needs.
   * Employees may take up to a fixed number of casual leave days per year as per company policy.

2. **Sick Leave (SL)**

   * Granted when an employee is unwell and unable to work.
   * Medical certificates may be required for extended sick leave.

3. **Earned Leave (EL) / Annual Leave**

   * Accumulated based on the number of working days completed.
   * Can be used for vacations or personal time off with prior approval.

4. **Maternity Leave**

   * Provided to female employees as per applicable labor laws.
   * Duration and conditions are governed by legal requirements.

5. **Paternity Leave**

   * Provided to male employees for the birth of a child, subject to company policy.

6. **Compensatory Leave (Comp-Off)**

   * Granted when employees work on approved holidays or weekends.

7. **Unpaid Leave (Leave Without Pay – LWP)**

   * Granted when all other leave balances are exhausted or under special circumstances.

---

## **4.2 Leave Application Process**

Employees must follow a proper process to apply for leave:

* Submit a leave request through the company’s leave management system or HR department.
* Inform the reporting manager in advance for planned leave.
* In case of emergency leave, inform the manager as soon as possible.
* Leave is approved based on work requirements and availability of staff.
* Unauthorized leave may be treated as misconduct.

---

## **4.3 Attendance Rules**

XYZ Company requires employees to maintain regular attendance and punctuality.

* Employees must mark attendance daily using the approved attendance system.
* Biometric or digital attendance methods may be used.
* Employees should report to work on time according to assigned shift schedules.
* Failure to mark attendance may result in salary deduction or disciplinary action.

---

## **4.4 Late Arrival and Early Departure**

* Employees arriving late or leaving early without permission will be recorded.
* Repeated late arrivals may lead to warnings or salary deductions.
* Permission must be taken from the reporting manager for early departure.
* Habitual late coming is considered a violation of company discipline.

---

## **4.5 Absenteeism**

Absence without prior approval or proper notification is considered unauthorized absenteeism.

Consequences may include:

* Salary deduction for the absent day(s).
* Warning letters.
* Disciplinary action in repeated cases.

Employees must inform HR or their manager if they are unable to attend work due to emergency situations.

---

## **4.6 Public Holidays and Weekly Offs**

* XYZ Company observes official public holidays as declared by management.
* The list of holidays is shared at the beginning of each year.
* Employees are entitled to weekly offs as per their work schedule.
* Working on holidays requires prior approval and may be eligible for compensatory leave.

---

## **4.7 Leave Encashment**

* Earned leave may be encashed as per company rules and legal requirements.
* Leave encashment is usually processed at the time of resignation or retirement.
* Approval from HR and finance department is required.

---

## **4.8 Record Maintenance**

* All leave and attendance records are maintained by the HR department.
* Employees can view their leave balance through the HR system or by contacting HR.
* Any discrepancies must be reported immediately for correction.

---

## **4.9 Special Leave**

XYZ Company may grant special leave for:

* Bereavement (death in family)
* Marriage
* Jury duty or legal obligations
* Company-sponsored events or training

Such leave is granted based on management discretion and company policy.

---

## **4.10 Policy Compliance**

All employees must comply with the Leave & Attendance Policy.

Failure to follow the policy may result in:

* Loss of pay
* Disciplinary action
* Impact on performance appraisal

XYZ Company reserves the right to modify the leave policy as per business and legal requirements.

## **12.5 Communication Channels**

Official communication is done through:

* Company email
* Internal messaging system
* Notice boards
* Company portal or intranet

Employees must regularly check official communication channels for updates.

---

## **12.6 Policy Updates and Notifications**

* Any changes to company policies will be communicated through official channels.
* Employees are responsible for staying updated with the latest policies.
